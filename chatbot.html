End-to-End NLP Chatbot for Food Delivery System - *(In Progress)*

This project involved the creation of an NLP-driven chatbot designed to enhance the operational efficiency of a food delivery service. Utilizing Dialogflow for natural language processing, Python and FastAPI for backend development, and MySQL for data management, the chatbot facilitates seamless order placement and real-time tracking, directly on the food service's website.

January 25, 2024 Jino anbu

Objective: To develop a user-friendly chatbot that understands and processes customer inquiries and orders, improving the user experience and reducing the need for manual customer service.
